Our Mission

Origin

This company was started by former chefs who truly understand the value of good knives. We believe that knives are the backbone of a good meal and should last a lifetime.

Craftsmanship

In an effort to create the highest quality knives, we work with skilled artisans from around the world. We pay our employees a fair wage for their unparalleled talents. Our metal is premium grade, stain-free, and meant to stand the test of time.

Customer Service

Because we believe so strongly in the quality of our knives, we offer a lifetime money-back guarantee. We also offer a knife sharpening subscription service, to keep you on the cutting edge in the kitchen.
